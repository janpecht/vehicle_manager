# Public Access

Certain areas of the application are accessible without login.

## Checklist Form

The checklist form is publicly accessible via QR code or direct link:

- URL: `/checklist/{VEHICLE-ID}` (with preselected vehicle)
- URL: `/checklist` (without preselection)

### Language Switcher

In the top right corner, you can switch between **German** and **English**.

### Form Fields

The form includes the following sections:

1. **Driver** -- Selection from active drivers
2. **Vehicle** -- Selection from active vehicles (may be preselected)
3. **Mileage** -- Current odometer reading
4. **Damage Status** -- Selection with preview of all four vehicle views:
   - New damage
   - Known damage
   - No damage
5. **Photos** (only for "New damage") -- Camera capture or file selection
6. **Dashboard Warnings** -- Checkboxes for oil, AdBlue, other warnings
7. **Condition** -- Yes/No selection for seats, smoking, food leftovers, cargo area
8. **Vehicle Systems** -- Cooling, charging cables
9. **Optional Fields** -- Delivery notes, fuel level, car wash needed
10. **Notes** -- Free text field (max 1000 characters)

### Submission

Click the submit button. After successful submission, a confirmation page appears with the option to fill out a new checklist.

> For critical entries (e.g., "New damage"), a notification email is automatically sent.

## Public Damage Report

The damage report for a vehicle is publicly accessible:

- URL: `/report/vehicles/{ID}`
- Shows all active damages across all four views
- Includes a print button
