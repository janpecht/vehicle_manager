# Damage Repair

Repaired damages can be marked as resolved without deleting them. This preserves the damage history.

## Mark as Repaired

1. Open the vehicle detail page.
2. Click on the damage marking on the canvas.
3. In the detail popup, click **Als repariert markieren** (Mark as repaired).
4. Confirm the action in the confirmation dialog.

## Appearance of Repaired Damages

Repaired damages are visually distinguished:

- **Dashed outline** instead of a solid line
- **Transparent fill** instead of opaque color
- The **repair date** is shown in the detail popup

## Filter

Use the **Reparierte anzeigen** (Show repaired) toggle in the toolbar to show or hide repaired damages. By default, only active damages are displayed.

## Delete a Damage

Active (non-repaired) damages can be deleted via the detail popup:

1. Click on the marking.
2. Click **Loschen** (Delete).
3. Confirm the deletion.

> Note: Deleting removes the damage permanently. Use "Mark as repaired" instead to preserve the history.
