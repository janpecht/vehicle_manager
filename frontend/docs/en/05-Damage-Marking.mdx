# Damage Marking

Damage marking is the core feature of the application. On an interactive canvas, you can mark damages directly on the vehicle silhouette.

## Open the View

1. Click **Anzeigen** (Show) on a vehicle in the vehicle list.
2. You will see the detail page with the interactive canvas.

## Four Views

Use tabs to switch between the four vehicle views:

- **FRONT** -- Front view
- **REAR** -- Rear view
- **LEFT** -- Left side
- **RIGHT** -- Right side

Each tab displays the number of active damages for that view.

## Tools

The toolbar offers two shape tools:

- **Circle** -- for point damages (default)
- **Rectangle** -- for area damages

## Mark a Damage

1. Select the desired tool (circle or rectangle).
2. Click and drag on the canvas to draw the shape.
3. After releasing, the **damage form** opens.
4. Fill in the form:
   - **Description** (optional, max 500 characters)
   - **Severity**: Low (yellow), Medium (orange), High (red)
5. Click **Speichern** (Save).

The damage appears immediately on the canvas, color-coded by severity.

## Move a Damage

Existing markings can be repositioned via drag and drop. The new position is saved automatically.

## View Damage Details

Click on an existing marking to open a detail popup. It shows:

- Severity (with color code)
- Shape (circle/rectangle)
- Status (active/repaired)
- Description (if provided)
- Creation date
- Repair date (if repaired)

## Color Coding

| Severity | Color |
|---|---|
| Low | Yellow |
| Medium | Orange |
| High | Red |

Repaired damages are displayed with a dashed outline and transparent fill.
