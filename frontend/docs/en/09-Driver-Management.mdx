# Driver Management

Driver management allows you to create and manage drivers who appear in the checklist forms.

## Driver List

Navigate to **Fahrer** (Drivers) in the main navigation. The table shows:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Driver name |
| **Status** | Active or Inactive |
| **Actions** | Edit, Activate/Deactivate, Delete |

### Filter

Enable **Inaktive Fahrer anzeigen** (Show inactive drivers) to display deactivated drivers.

## Create a Driver

1. Click **Fahrer hinzufugen** (Add driver).
2. Enter the driver's **name**.
3. Click **Speichern** (Save).

## Edit a Driver

Click **Bearbeiten** (Edit) in the actions column to change the name.

## Deactivate a Driver

Click **Deaktivieren** (Deactivate). Deactivated drivers no longer appear in the checklist form.

## Delete a Driver

Click **Loschen** (Delete) and confirm the action.

> Note: Drivers that are already referenced in checklist submissions cannot be deleted. Deactivate them instead.
