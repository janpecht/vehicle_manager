# Authentication

## Registration

1. Open the **registration page** via the link on the login page.
2. Enter your **name**, **email address**, and a **password**.
3. The password must be at least 8 characters long and contain uppercase letters, lowercase letters, and digits.
4. After registration, a **6-digit verification code** is sent to your email.

> Note: Only email addresses from the company domain can register.

## Email Verification

1. Enter the 6-digit code you received by email.
2. The input fields advance automatically. You can also paste the code (Ctrl+V).
3. If the code didn't arrive, click **Resend code** (available after 60 seconds).
4. After successful verification, you will be redirected to the login page.

## Login

1. Enter your **email address** and **password**.
2. After successful login, you will be taken to the vehicle list.
3. Your session remains active and is automatically refreshed in the background.

## Forgot Password

1. Click **Forgot password?** on the login page.
2. Enter your email address.
3. You will receive a **6-digit reset code** by email (valid for 15 minutes).
4. Enter the code and your new password on the reset page.
5. After resetting, all existing sessions are terminated.

## Logout

Click **Abmelden** (Logout) in the top right corner to sign out.
